Teacher, graphic artist, scientist...I've had an eclectic professional background, and have gotten a lot out of my diverse experiences. I was drawn to education, specifically Montessori education, because of my passion for the mission of teaching to the whole child in a develomentally appropriate way. As someone who loves learning, it was important to me that I work within an education philosophy that would cultivate that same love in my students, while emphasizing social/emotional skills. I knew I was making a positive impact, and loved the camaraderie I felt in my co-teaching team, as well as in my department.

I have been an artist my whole life, and will always need a creative outlet. I have designed logos internationally, and sold my art all over the world. Finding the most elegant solution to convey my clients' business mission and personality is extremely fun and satisfying for me.

I have a strong science background as well. I was nominated to be the student leader of the Brain and Language Lab at DePaul University and received a grant to pursue research on how the mind processes insight. I am very analytical, and enjoy reasoning through complex problems in a logical, clever way.

As a web developer, I will get to combine all of the things I have loved about my previous career paths. I can be an empathetic teammate, a creative coder, and a clever problem solver – all while learning something new every day!
